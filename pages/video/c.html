Mary Gordon:

Mary Gordon, is a Canadian educator, author, child advocate, parenting expert and award-winning social entrepreneur, who believes in the power of empathy. She is the founder of Roots of Empathy, an organization that brings babies into classrooms to teach emotional literacy to children as a way of combatting bullying. In this GROOC interview she describes the impact of the program and offers advice about perseverance to budding social entrepreneurs.
